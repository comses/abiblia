CoMSES Catalog

The CoMSES Catalog app is being developed to discover and improve the state of computational model archiving. The digital context, e.g., metadata, code, documentation, and data needed for reproducible research are often missing from publications that depend on computational models or other software artifacts.

We are compiling a list of publications that depend on computational models across several journals and tagging their research sponsor, contact addresses, and whether or not their digital context has been archived in a digital repository or otherwise available online.
